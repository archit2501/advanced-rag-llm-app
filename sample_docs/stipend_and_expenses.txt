STIPEND AND REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
Finance Office | Policy FIN-AIRI-2026 | Effective 1 April 2026

Each full-time intern receives a stipend of INR 45,000 per month for the twelve-week programme. Finance pays three equal monthly instalments after receipt of the intern's bank and tax forms. The stipend is taxable according to the intern's circumstances; the university cannot give personal tax advice.

Housing and meals are not included. A domestic round trip to the university may be reimbursed up to INR 12,000 when the programme coordinator approves the travel in writing before booking. Local commuting, international travel, visa charges, and optional conference travel are not reimbursed under this policy. Original receipts must be submitted within ten working days after travel.

Approved research expenses such as participant compensation, dataset access, or cloud credits are charged to the lab budget and require mentor approval before purchase. Interns must not pay research participants from personal funds.
