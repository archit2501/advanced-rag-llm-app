WORK SCHEDULE, ATTENDANCE, AND LEAVE
Programme Office | Version 4.0 | Summer 2026

The standard commitment is 35 hours per week. All interns attend on campus on Tuesday and Thursday. Work on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday may be remote when the mentor agrees and project needs allow. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 15:00 IST. Required seminars and participant sessions may occasionally fall outside core hours with advance notice.

Interns receive two personal-leave days during the programme. Personal leave should be requested at least two working days in advance. For illness lasting more than two consecutive working days, the intern must contact the programme office and provide the documentation required by university policy. Public holidays in the university calendar do not consume personal leave.

Leave is normally not approved for the final demonstration on 21 August 2026. An emergency exception requires the programme lead's approval, and the intern must arrange a recorded demonstration within five working days. Attendance is measured by agreed work and participation, not by continuous webcam monitoring.
