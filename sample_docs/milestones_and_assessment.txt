MILESTONES AND ASSESSMENT STANDARD
Academic Committee | Summer 2026

The project charter is due Friday, 5 June. The literature review is due 19 June. The data-management and ethics plan is due 26 June. A working prototype and design review are due 17 July. The frozen evaluation package, including test questions and baseline outputs, is due 7 August. The final written report and reproducibility appendix are due Monday, 17 August. The poster, repository release, and live demonstration are due Friday, 21 August.

In addition to those formal milestones, every intern posts a weekly progress report by 17:00 IST each Friday. The weekly report is a short status update and is distinct from the final written report. If a question refers only to "the report," staff should clarify whether it means a weekly progress report or the final written report.

Assessment weights are: research framing 20 percent, technical implementation 25 percent, evaluation quality 25 percent, reproducibility 15 percent, and communication 15 percent. A mentor and an independent reviewer score the work. Passing requires an overall score of at least 60 percent and no finding of serious ethics or security non-compliance.
