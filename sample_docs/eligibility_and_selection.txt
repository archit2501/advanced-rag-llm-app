ELIGIBILITY AND SELECTION POLICY
Admissions Committee | Effective for Summer 2026

Applicants must be enrolled in a master's degree at an accredited university throughout the application period. Eligible fields include computer science, data science, statistics, electrical engineering, computational linguistics, information systems, and a related quantitative discipline approved by the committee. Undergraduate and doctoral students are not eligible for this master's internship track.

Before the internship begins, an applicant must have completed one graduate machine-learning course or demonstrate equivalent research experience. Working knowledge of Python is required. Prior publication, prior RAG experience, and a Graduate Record Examination (GRE) score are not required.

Selection uses four equally disclosed factors: research-statement quality, evidence of technical preparation, alignment with an available mentor, and interview performance. Two reviewers independently score each application. A third reviewer adjudicates a difference greater than 20 points on the 100-point rubric. Applicants may request an accessibility accommodation without disclosing medical details to reviewers.
